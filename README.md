🏨 Hotel Booking – Revenue & Cancellation Risk Analysis (SQL Project)

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel booking data to uncover insights related to revenue performance, cancellation risk, customer loyalty, pricing strategy, and revenue concentration.

Using advanced SQL techniques, the project evaluates booking behavior, seasonal profitability, distribution channel efficiency, and financial risk patterns to support data-driven decision-making for hotel management.

The analysis focuses on identifying revenue drivers, minimizing cancellation losses, and improving overall operational strategy.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to:

Analyze overall hotel revenue performance

Measure and reduce cancellation risk

Evaluate pricing strategy using ADR (Average Daily Rate)

Identify customer loyalty impact on profitability

Detect revenue concentration and dependency risks

Understand seasonal and lead-time booking behavior

📊 Business Questions Answered

1️⃣ Total Revenue & Booking Demand

Measures total bookings and realized revenue (excluding cancellations) to evaluate demand performance.

2️⃣ Overall Cancellation Rate

Calculates hotel-wide cancellation percentage to measure operational risk.

3️⃣ Revenue by Hotel Type

Identifies which hotel category (City vs Resort) generates higher revenue.

4️⃣ Revenue by Market Segment

Analyzes which customer segments (Online TA, Direct, Corporate, Groups, etc.) contribute most to total revenue.

5️⃣ Pricing Strategy – ADR Analysis

Compares Average Daily Rate across customer types to understand pricing effectiveness.

6️⃣ Top Revenue-Generating Countries

Identifies the top 5 countries contributing highest revenue.

7️⃣ Monthly Revenue Trend

Analyzes seasonality and monthly revenue patterns.

8️⃣ Cancellation Risk by Hotel & Market Segment

Identifies high-risk booking channels and segments with elevated cancellation rates.

9️⃣ Revenue Leakage Detection

Finds long stays generating below-average revenue, highlighting pricing inefficiencies.

🔟 Seasonal Profitability Analysis

Compares revenue, ADR, and cancellation rates across seasons.

1️⃣1️⃣ Customer Loyalty Impact

Compares repeated vs new guests on:

Average revenue per booking

Cancellation rate

Booking volume

1️⃣2️⃣ Lead Time Risk Analysis

Analyzes how advance booking days influence cancellation probability.

1️⃣3️⃣ Revenue Concentration Risk

Calculates what percentage of total revenue comes from the top 10% highest-value bookings.

1️⃣4️⃣ Weekend vs Weekday Revenue Contribution

Measures revenue split between weekday and weekend stays.

1️⃣5️⃣ Distribution Channel Performance

Identifies channels with:

High ADR

Low cancellation rate

Strong booking volume

1️⃣6️⃣ Revenue Loss from No Deposit Bookings

Estimates potential revenue lost due to cancellations without deposits.

🔍 Key Insights

Online Travel Agencies contribute the highest revenue share.

No-deposit bookings have significantly higher cancellation rates.

Longer lead times increase cancellation probability.

Revenue is concentrated among top 10% of bookings, indicating dependency risk.

Repeated guests generate more stable revenue with lower cancellation rates.

Weekend revenue contributes around one-third of total revenue.

Seasonal demand peaks during specific high-travel months.
